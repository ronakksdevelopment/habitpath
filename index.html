📲 Install HabitPath
🤖
Android · Chrome Browser
Works on Chrome, Brave, Edge for Android
1
Open in Chrome
Make sure you're using Google Chrome browser on your Android device.
2
Tap the Menu (⋮)
Tap the three-dot menu in the top-right corner of Chrome.
⋮ Menu → …
3
Tap "Add to Home screen"
Select "Add to Home screen" from the dropdown menu. A dialog will appear.
📲 Add to Home screen
4
Tap "Add"
Confirm by tapping Add. HabitPath icon appears on your home screen!
💡 Tip: You may also see an automatic "Install app" banner at the bottom of Chrome — just tap it!
🍎
iPhone / iPad · Safari
Must use Safari — other browsers don't support install on iOS
1
Open in Safari
Copy this page's URL and open it in Safari browser (not Chrome or Firefox).
2
Tap the Share Button
At the bottom of Safari, tap the Share icon (box with an arrow pointing up).
⬆️ Share button
3
Scroll & Tap "Add to Home Screen"
In the share sheet, scroll down to find and tap "Add to Home Screen".
＋ Add to Home Screen
4
Tap "Add"
Confirm with Add in the top-right. HabitPath launches like a native app!
💡 Note: On iOS 16.4+, you get full offline support. On older iOS, it still works great as a home screen shortcut!
🖥️
Windows / Mac / Linux · Chrome
Google Chrome or Brave on desktop
1
Look for the Install Icon
In Chrome's address bar (URL bar), look for a computer/install icon (⊕) on the right side.
⊕ Install icon in address bar
2
Click "Install HabitPath"
Click the icon and then click "Install" in the popup.
3
App opens in its own window
HabitPath opens as a standalone app without browser chrome. A shortcut is added to your desktop/taskbar.
💡 Alternative: Chrome menu (⋮) → Save and share → Install page as app
🌐
Microsoft Edge · Desktop or Mobile
Windows 10/11, macOS, Android, iOS
1
Click the Edge Menu (…)
Click the three-dot menu (…) in the top-right corner of Microsoft Edge.
2
Select "Apps"
Hover over Apps in the dropdown menu.
… → Apps
3
Click "Install this site as an app"
Click Install this site as an app and confirm with Install.
📲 Install this site as an app
💡 Edge also shows an install icon in the address bar — click it for the fastest install!
📱
Samsung Internet Browser
Galaxy phones & tablets
1
Open Samsung Internet
Open this page in the Samsung Internet browser app on your Galaxy device.
2
Tap the Menu (☰)
Tap the three-line menu icon at the bottom-right of the browser.
☰ Menu
3
Tap "Add page to" → "Home screen"
Select Add page to, then choose Home screen and tap Add.
+ Add page to → Home screen
💡 Samsung Internet also supports Web App installs — look for a "Install" banner that may appear automatically!
